## Why Differentiating Row Vector and Column Vector?

One of the most basic operation we learnt in our first linear algebra courses must be the "dot product" $$ u^Tv $$. However, have you wondered why differentiating "row" vector and "column" vector here? Moreover, why placing "row" vector on the left (otherwise the result is a matrix other than a scalar)? Is it just to conform the definition of matrix multiplication?

The answer is that "row" vector and "column" vector are totally different, namely "covector" and "vector".
